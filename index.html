Welcome to KissanSardhak, our e-marketplace platform for dairy and poultry farmers and businesses. Our platform aims to connect buyers and sellers in a secure and efficient manner, providing a convenient and accessible way for businesses and individuals to transact and exchange goods and services.

How to's of KisaanSardhak

What are fees and document required.

  • There is no fee for registration on KisaanSardhak?
  • Details required : mandatory details like Name, Sex, Address, DOB, Mobile Number, Bank details etc.
  • Documents required : Documents like Passbook (Cheque leaf), Any Govt Identity proof etc.

What are benefits for a farmer?

  • Transparency in Trade through better price discovery
  • Access to more markets & buyers
  • Real time information on prices and arrival in near by mandis

How do I get better price?

  • KisaanSardhak facilitates better prices for a seller through transparent bidding , increased number of buyers from different markets , hence greater negotiation power. Via assaying, price commensurate to the quality of produce is obtained by the seller.
